About Us

Who is Sociara?

Sociara is a creative social media and digital marketing agency that helps brands stand out in today’s crowded digital space. We specialize in video shoots, video editing, and social-first storytelling, while also providing end-to-end marketing solutions including content strategy, design, and ads.

From lifestyle and fitness brands like Vault by Virat Kohli and Muscle & Music Gym, to popular cafes like Jungle Jamboree and even astrology businesses, our work spans across industries with one common goal—helping our clients grow online and build lasting connections with their audience.
